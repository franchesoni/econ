
### Fiscal Logic

1. **Unified tax rule for all citizens** — workers, owners, and investors follow the same accounting logic.
2. **Tax base = net profit** — income minus all expenses (no distinction between consumption and investment).
3. **Wealth tax** — self-declared, backed by incentives to honesty; complements income taxation to prevent indefinite accumulation.
4. **External boundary** — tariffs and exit taxes capture value leaving the economy.
5. **Dynamic management** — state adjusts transfers, tariffs, or interest to stabilize inflation and demand.
6. **Reinvestment advantage** — any reinvested or productively used capital enjoys full deductibility or deferral.

---

### Fiscal Purpose

The revenue funds:

1. **Basic welfare and healthcare**
2. **Law and justice**
3. **Education and human capital**
4. **Infrastructure**
5. **Regulation and environmental balance**
6. **Cultural cohesion**
7. **Foreign relations and diplomacy**
8. **Strategic national investment via a sovereign wealth fund**

---

In short: **tax net extraction, not activity; treat everyone symmetrically; and use the proceeds to secure, empower, and modernize society while compounding collective capital.**
